REBECCA KANTER

Hello, I'm Rebecca Kanter.
I am a musician, scientist, engineer, campaign staffer, and junior at LaGuardia High School of the Arts.
Welcome to my website!

Music:

  • I have been playing flute since first grade. Since then, I have picked up the oboe, violin, piccolo, and english horn.
  • I play in the Interschool Orchestra (ISO) Symphony and the New York Youth Symphonic Band (formerly known as the ISO Wind Symphony). I also play in the Junior Band at LaGuardia High School.
  • I have performed at Symphony Space, Jazz at Lincoln Center, the Hunter Kaye Playhouse, the Met Museum, the Brooklyn Museum, and Carnegie Hall.
